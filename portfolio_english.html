About me

    • CV

  • I am a Ph.D student at Toyota Technological Institute (Japan)． I'm now at Intelligent Information Media Lab working with Prof. Normichi Ukita.

    My research interest includes action recognition, especially in the context of group activity understanding.

    I've published papers on joint attention estimation (ICCV2023), individual and group activity recognition (CVPR2024, MVA2023, MVA2021), and gaze estimation (MVA2023).

    I'm looking for a research internship. If you are interested in my research, please feel free to contact me. (E-mail: sd23501 [at] toyota-ti.ac.jp)
